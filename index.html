About

Matthew joined Amazon Web Services as a member of the seventh TechU cohort prior to joining the Central SA team, where he supported public sector customers as a Solutions Architect. During that time he began a Vertical Apprenticeship with the Aerospace & Satellite industry team, helping customers find solutions such as: management of geospatial data, HPC designs for performing computational fluid dynamics, and ground station operations, to name a few. Matthew currently works to help partners grow by developing solutions tailored for Aerospace & Satellite customers.

In 2019, Matthew graduated from the University of South Florida and majored in Information Studies with a Concentration in Date Science & Analytics.

Matthew is an experienced web design specialist and runs his own web design consulting business during his free time. Some of his clients include Clinical Pharmacy Partners, Team Florida Gymnastics, Portland State University I-O Psychology Lab, and the GroDev YMCA Annual Summit.
